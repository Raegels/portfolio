About me


Theatraite

For my first client I was asked to deliver a website that the client could alter, adjust and maintain themselves as much as possible. Due to the nature of the request I chose WordPress as a base for the site.

Theatraite is a company that hosts silent and theatre retreats in the Netherlands. I did an intake with the owner to get a feel for the branding, most of which had yet to be determined. I designed the website to have colors that give an earthy feel and used a lot of nature elements.

Even though I barely needed to code anything, I found it an enlightening experience to build a website from the ground up. I gave me my first experience working with a client and streamlining the process of delivery and adjustments.

Another thing that was actually a big learning point for me is that the process of designing is not where my passion lies. Even though I enjoyed the project because the subject matter was really interesting, I really enjoy coding and implementing logic. I have added this project to my portfolio for this reason. After this project I knew I needed to pivot to coding completely to have a fulfilling career.

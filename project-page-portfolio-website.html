About me


Portfolio Website

I knew right away that I wanted my portfolio to represent a little bit of me. I was playing Super Mario Bros. on the NES when I suddenly thought: Wouldn’t it be cool to have a Mario themed page?

And that’s how it started!

I sat down with my sketchbook and started drawing some elements I knew my portfolio needed to have and some elements I knew I wanted to have to give it some extra flair.

As of right now, May 2024, the project consists only of HTML and CSS. I started with the intention of putting a whole bunch of JavaScript in there but it really wasn’t necessary. The solutions were often easier to implement in CSS. Even if I would’ve maybe liked it to be different in the beginning, from a usefulness and efficiency standpoint I couldn’t justify it.

I quite happy with how it turned out thus far, even if there are some changes to be made. In the future I would love for a Mario sprite to actually enter the tunnels and jump up against the coin boxes. That’ll definitely challenge my JavaScript skills.

For now though, I hope you enjoy my Mario Portfolio!
